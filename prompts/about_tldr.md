# About TLDR

## Company Overview
TLDR is a suite of free daily newsletters covering tech, software development, AI, and business topics. We reach over 5 million subscribers across our newsletter portfolio.

## What We Sell
We sell **premium advertising placements** (sponsorships) within our newsletters to enterprises and growth-stage companies looking to reach technical and business audiences.

## Newsletter Portfolio
- **TLDR (Main)** - Daily tech news for software engineers and tech workers (1.25M+ subscribers)
- **TLDR Web Dev** - Frontend and full-stack development (350K+ subscribers)
- **TLDR DevOps** - Infrastructure, cloud, and platform engineering (250K+ subscribers)
- **TLDR AI** - Artificial intelligence and machine learning (550K+ subscribers)
- **TLDR InfoSec** - Cybersecurity and security engineering (200K+ subscribers)
- **TLDR Founders** - Startup and business content for technical founders (300K+ subscribers)
- **TLDR PM** - Product management (150K+ subscribers)
- **TLDR Marketing** - Growth and marketing for tech companies (150K+ subscribers)

## Our Value Proposition
1. **Highly engaged technical audience** - Our readers are developers, engineering leaders, and technical decision-makers who actively build and buy software
2. **Intent-rich context** - Readers come to learn about new tools and technologies, making them receptive to relevant product discovery
3. **Trusted placement** - Ads appear as curated recommendations within editorial content, not disruptive banner ads
4. **Measurable results** - We provide detailed analytics on impressions, clicks, and engagement

## Ideal Customers
- Developer tools companies (databases, APIs, DevOps platforms)
- B2B SaaS targeting technical buyers
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Security and compliance tools
- AI/ML platforms and tools
- Technical recruiting and hiring platforms

## Typical Engagement
- Single newsletter sponsorship: 1-4 week campaigns
- Multi-newsletter bundles: Reach across multiple audience segments
- Premium placements: Top-of-newsletter featured spots
- Ongoing partnerships: Quarterly or annual commitments
