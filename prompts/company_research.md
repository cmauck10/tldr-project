# Company Research Prompt

You are researching a company to help TLDR's sales team understand if they're a good prospect for newsletter advertising.

## Research Objectives
Gather the following information about the target company:

### 1. Company Basics
- What does the company do? (one sentence)
- What is their primary product or service?
- Who is their target customer?

### 2. Industry & Market Position
- What industry/vertical are they in?
- Are they a startup, growth-stage, or enterprise company?
- Who are their main competitors?

### 3. Technical Audience Relevance
- Do they sell to developers, engineers, or technical buyers?
- What technical personas would care about their product?
- Which TLDR newsletters would their audience read?

### 4. Buying Signals
- Any recent funding rounds or growth news?
- New product launches that need awareness?
- Hiring trends (are they scaling marketing/growth teams)?
- Any existing advertising or content marketing presence?

### 5. Potential Angles
- What pain points might they be trying to solve with advertising?
- What would "success" look like for them with TLDR?

## Output Format
Provide a structured summary with clear sections. Be concise but thorough. Flag any information you couldn't find or are uncertain about.
